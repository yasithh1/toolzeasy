About

Simple tools for everyday files and text

ToolzEasy helps people finish common tasks quickly: combine PDFs, resize images, count words, generate QR codes, calculate values, and download useful results.

Our goal

The goal is to keep small digital tasks easy. You should not need a login, a dashboard, or a complicated app just to convert a file, check text, or create a quick utility result.

How tools work

Many tools run directly in your browser. Advanced PDF features use browser libraries and may depend on the file format, password restrictions, browser support, and file size.

Privacy approach

ToolzEasy is built without accounts or saved uploads. For most tools, the file you choose stays on your device and is processed locally in the browser.
